Seğmenler Park

Enjoy culture in this vibrant park close to the center of Ankara.

Top three activities to do at Seğmenler

Go for a hike

Its a little bit of nature inside the capital to get away and hike.

Relax by the fountain

Enjoy a nice beverage, go for a piknic or just chill with your friends by the water.

Around the park

Just outside of the park, there are various malls, stores and vibrant nightlife with lots of pubs and interesting people.
